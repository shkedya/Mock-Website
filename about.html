About Us

Alon's School is an international public research university that brings in people from all 50 states and over 100 countries. We are able to conduct school in a formal or informal setting, this is usually done via ecampus schooling or oncampus schooling. We are a diverse and welcoming school that hope people that come to our campus build a future that's "smarter, healthier, more prosperous and more just." We are a new school so hopefully we get to see a large and diverse populous that comes!

  • 2 campuses - ecampus and otherwise
  • 1 college - ecampus and regular go to the same college
  • 5 experiment stations - we will hopefully build more!
  • Extension programs in most countries
  • 200+ academic programs that bring countless impacts
